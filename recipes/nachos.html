Nachos

Igredients

  • Butter and flour: This nacho cheese starts with a roux made of equal parts butter and all-purpose flour.
  • Milk: You'll need a cup of whole milk.
  • Cheese: For this classic nacho cheese recipe, opt for sliced processed cheese
  • Salt: A pinch of salt enhances the savory flavor of the nacho cheese.

Step by Step

  1. Make the roux with butter and flour.
  2. 2 tablespoons butter
  3. 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  4. Pour in the milk and whisk until thick
  5. 1 cup milk
  6. Add cheese and salt, then cook until the cheese is melted.
